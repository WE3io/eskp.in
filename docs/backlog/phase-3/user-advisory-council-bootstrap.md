# user-advisory-council-bootstrap

**Phase:** 3 — Governance and measurement
**Article:** 10.2.4 (User governance participation)

## Outcome

A User Advisory Council is established: a public `/governance` page describes the council's role, rights (per Article 10.2.4), and how to apply. An email address (council@eskp.in) is created for submissions. The first council is manually selected by Sunil from willing users and documented publicly. The council has a formal record in `docs/governance/`.

## Constraints & References

- Article 10.2.4: council must exist with binding input rights on pricing, data policy, core architecture; recommendations responded to within 30 days publicly; council may not be abolished without a constitutional amendment
- Requires at least a small active user base before meaningful selection
- `docs/governance/` directory to be created

## Acceptance Checks

- `/governance` page live and linked from the site footer
- `council@eskp.in` email routing confirmed and tested
- At least one council member appointed and documented in `docs/governance/council-001.md`
- Council record includes: member(s), remit, term dates, rights per Article 10.2.4
- Governance page states the 30-day response commitment explicitly

## Explicit Non-Goals

- Democratic election mechanism (manual selection sufficient for bootstrap)
- Formal veto powers (Article 10.2.4 specifies binding input, not veto)
- Paid council membership
