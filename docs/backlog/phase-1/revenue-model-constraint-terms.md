# revenue-model-constraint-terms

**Phase:** 1 — Foundational
**Article:** 10.2.1 (No advertiser class)
**Status:** done

## Outcome

The Terms of Service are updated to include an explicit, user-facing commitment that the platform will never sell user attention, behavioural data, or relational data to third parties, and that the only revenue model is direct user payment. The existing draft banner is removed once this clause (and any other outstanding legal items) receives sign-off from Sunil.

## Constraints & References

- Article 10.2.1: no advertiser class; any proposal for advertising triggers mandatory governance review
- Existing: `public/terms.html` (draft banner present, pending legal sign-off)
- Requires Sunil sign-off before draft banner is removed

## Acceptance Checks

- `public/terms.html` contains an explicit clause prohibiting sale of user attention, behavioural data, and relational data to third parties
- Clause references the platform's direct-payment-only revenue model
- Clause is in plain English and clearly visible in the terms
- Draft banner removed (or a separate task is logged for that pending legal sign-off)

## Explicit Non-Goals

- Legal review by a solicitor (flagged for Sunil/panel — separate task)
- CIC or social enterprise legal structuring (Phase 3)
