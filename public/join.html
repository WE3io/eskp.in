Become a helper.

Share what you know with someone who needs it. One email introduction, no platform to manage, no obligation beyond a first conversation.

How it works

When someone describes a goal that matches your background, we'll send you an email introduction. You can reply to connect, or ignore it if it's not a good fit. That's it.

We don't pay helpers and we don't charge users (yet). This is a small, early network built on the idea that most people who've solved hard problems are willing to spend an hour helping someone else do the same.

What kind of help is useful

Anything you've navigated yourself. Some examples:

Career Changing industries, negotiating a job offer, becoming a founder.
Legal Contracts, employment disputes, GDPR, company formation.
Technical Architecture decisions, stack choices, scaling problems.
Business Finding first customers, pricing, fundraising, co-founder dynamics.
Creative Shipping a product, writing, building an audience.
Other If you've solved something hard, it probably belongs here.
