Roadmap

What we've built, what's coming next, and what users have asked for.
Updated 2026-03-13.

Phase 1 — Core platform Live
✓
Submit a goal by email
Email hello@mail.eskp.in — we'll do the rest.
✓
AI goal decomposition
Your submission is structured into specific, actionable needs using Claude Haiku.
✓
Helper matching
Goals are matched to helpers by expertise. You see a summary before paying.
✓
Pay for an introduction (£10)
Stripe Checkout — one payment, one introduction.
✓
Clarification loop
If your goal is too vague, we ask focused questions before matching.
✓
Data export and account deletion
Email us to download your data or delete your account. Both are automated.
✓
Sensitive domain handling
Goals in health, legal, and financial domains are flagged and handled with extra care — including warm signposting to regulated services.
✓
Post-introduction follow-up
A check-in email 24 hours after an introduction, to make sure the connection is working.
✓
No-match timeout notification
If your goal hasn't been matched within 7 days, you'll get an honest update — with options to wait, reframe, or close.
✓
ICO registration
Registered with the ICO (C1889388). £52 annual data protection fee paid.
✓
Tag normalisation for helper onboarding
56 canonical expertise tags. Suggested during helper onboarding for better match quality.
✓
AI opt-out
Say "no AI" in your email and your goal is routed to a human without AI processing.
✓
Privacy policy signed off
Formally reviewed and approved. Draft banner removed.
