How it works

  1. Send an email

    Write to hello@mail.eskp.in and describe what you're trying to do — in plain language, as much or as little as you know. No template required.

  2. We structure your goal

    We use AI to break down what you actually need into specific, actionable parts, then reflect it back to you so you know we've understood correctly.

  3. We make an introduction

    You're connected by email with someone who has relevant experience. They've agreed to help — there's no obligation on either side beyond that first conversation.

What does a goal look like?

Write naturally — there's no template. Here are three examples of the kind of emails we receive.

Career
"I've been a backend developer for six years and want to move into product management. I'm not sure if my experience qualifies, how to position myself in interviews, or whether I should try to make the transition at my current company or find a new role first."
Business
"I'm building a small SaaS product for freelance designers and have about 40 users. I have no idea what to charge — I'm worried about pricing too high and losing people, but also that I'm leaving money on the table. I'd like to talk to someone who's been through this."
Technical
"I'm redesigning our data pipeline at work and need to decide between keeping everything in Postgres or moving some of it to a document store. The tradeoffs aren't obvious to me. I'd value a conversation with someone who's built something similar at scale."

What to expect

  • Within 24 hours You'll receive an acknowledgement email. Our AI will summarise what we think you're trying to do — so you can confirm we've understood correctly before we search for a match. If anything looks off, just reply.
  • Within a few days If we find a good match, our AI will identify the closest helper for your goal. We'll send an introduction email with their details and a £10 payment link. No charge if there's no match.
  • No match found We'll tell you honestly. We won't leave you waiting. If you're happy for us to keep your goal on file as the helper network grows, just say so.
  • After the intro The conversation is yours. There's no obligation on either side beyond that first exchange. Most people find one call is enough.

What if there’s no match?

We'll tell you within 24 hours if we can't find anyone for your goal. No charge. No silence.

If the network grows and a match becomes available, we'll reach out — or you can resubmit with a different framing. You're in control.

We're a small platform and we'd rather be honest about our limits than introduce you to the wrong person.

Honest about what this is

eskp.in is an early-stage platform. The helper network is small right now. We will only introduce you to someone when we think it's a genuinely good match — which means sometimes we'll say we don't have anyone yet.


A £10 fee applies when we make an introduction. There's no charge to submit a request or if we can't find a match.


The platform is being built in public. Goal decomposition is assisted by AI (Claude Haiku). Matching and introductions are reviewed before being sent. We don't sell your data. We don't use it for anything other than connecting you with help.
