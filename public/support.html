Support Resources

Sometimes a platform introduction is not what you need right now. This page lists specialist services across a range of areas — all free to contact, available to anyone in the UK, and staffed by people trained for exactly these situations. You do not need to use eskp.in to access any of these services.

If you are in immediate danger, call 999. For urgent non-emergency situations, call 111. eskp.in is a peer advisory platform, not an emergency service.

Mental health crisis

  • Samaritans
    116 123 (free, 24/7)  ·  jo@samaritans.org
  • Shout Crisis Text Service
    Text SHOUT to 85258 (free, 24/7)
  • NHS 111
    111 — urgent mental health support, 24/7
  • Mind
    0300 123 3393  ·  mind.org.uk
  • CALM
    0800 58 58 58 (5pm–midnight) — for anyone feeling low or in crisis
